# AdaptyvBio

## What It Is
[AdaptyvBio](https://www.adaptyvbio.com/) is a platform that bridges computational protein design and wet-bench production. It helps scientists who lack in-house computational expertise turn in silico protein designs into real molecules. The platform leverages tools like [[BindCraft]], [[BoltzGen]], and [[Rosetta]] for the computational design phase, then handles the expression and validation work.

## What I Know
- Service model: scientists submit design objectives, AdaptyvBio handles computational design and production[^1]
- Planning to use this for [[Extracellular Targeted Protein Degradation|extracellular TPD]] protein binders targeting [[NaV1.8]] and [[NaV1.9]][^2]
- Currently collaborating with someone at Washington University on computational protein design tools in parallel[^2]
