# NaV1.9

## What It Is
NaV1.9 (encoded by SCN11A) is a voltage-gated sodium channel expressed in dorsal root ganglion (DRG) neurons. It produces a persistent, slowly inactivating current that sets the resting membrane potential and modulates excitability in nociceptors. Like [[NaV1.8]], it is a pain target, though it has been harder to drug due to its biophysical properties.
